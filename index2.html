Concept

Paramecias display a behaviour known as galvanotaxis: movement resulting a response directly to an electrical field.

If an electrical field is generated across the water while the Paramecias swimming in the fluid, they will turn to orient themselves to the charged field and will swim towards the cathode (-).
- Geva Patz, 25 Apr 2013
